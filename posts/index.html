AmplifyHER

August 4, 2024

I created AmplifyHER in UF’s 2024 WingHacks along with my partner Karlina Maya. Our website prompts the user to pick an option from a list of preselected genres to give them a new playlist. It filters through a gender database that provides us with information about artists gender identity and made it so that only female, non-binary, and GNC individuals would be added to the generated playlists. We utilized a combination of JavaScript, HTML/CSS, and Python in order to create our website.
